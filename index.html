My why

The technology has never been the point.

What moves me are the people living through this shift — the teams afraid of being replaced, the children growing up in a world reshaped by AI. I’ve spent my life making sure AI amplifies what makes us human instead of erasing it.

That’s why I build, I teach, and I speak — all in service of one goal: to use technology to impact a billion lives for the better.
