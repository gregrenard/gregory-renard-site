Cognitive Orchestration™

From AI ambition to operational reality.

Most organizations stall at the same place: pilots multiply, budgets grow, yet AI never reaches production with real ROI. After 30 years building applied AI at global scale, I turned that recurring failure into a method — Cognitive Orchestration™ — a proven three-phase path from AI ambition to systems live in production, with measurable impact and governance built in.

Method successfully deployed with Groupe Eiffage, Sodebo, Actual, Groupe IDEA, AAICO (now Opus) & many more

The problem ↓ The solution ↓ The essay & podcast ↓
The shift

AI is no longer just a tool.

It is becoming an operational layer integrated into workflows, decision systems, and organizational memory. The challenge is no longer access to AI — it is the ability to operationalize it meaningfully, safely, and at scale.

The problem

Most companies never get AI into production — let alone a positive ROI.

Pilots multiply, budgets grow — and AI stays stuck in slideware and endless POCs. The gap is rarely the technology. It's the absence of a method that connects executive vision, real data, governance and adoption.

Stalled POCs

Promising pilots that never ship to production or scale.

No measurable ROI

AI spend with no clear, audited business impact.

No governance

Black-box tools — compliance and adoption left to chance.

AI transformation is already here. The real question: do you have a partner who has lived it for 30 years — and thought deeply about what it does to work? Read the essays & listen to the podcast →

The solution

Cognitive Orchestration™ — from ambition to production

A proven, end-to-end method — refined over 30 years and deployed at global scale — taking you from ambition to AI live in production.

Phase 1
Weeks 1–3

Understand, Frame & Align Leadership

Before building anything, we answer the most important question: why. Why AI, why now, and what kind of company you want to become. Through executive workshops we clarify the vision, establish the cultural and strategic foundations, and — using our FAI-Score™ readiness diagnostic — map where AI can create tangible, measurable value.

OUTCOME Clarity of purpose, leadership alignment, and a strategic north star for AI that inspires and drives action.
Phase 2
8–10 weeks

Co-Design, Prototype & Deliver Quick Wins

AI stops being a concept and becomes a measurable driver of performance. In hands-on qualification workshops (business + IT) we isolate high-ROI opportunities and deploy 2–3 operational AI co-workers or agentic workflows, built on your own data and systems.

  • ▸Business-process mapping and data-readiness check
  • ▸Co-creation of use cases with your teams (Finance, HR, Ops, Supply Chain…)
  • ▸Secure RAGs, copilots and automation pipelines deployed in your environment
  • ▸Embedded observability, governance and compliance from day one
  • ▸“Before / After” KPI dashboard — time saved, accuracy, adoption
OUTCOME Measurable business impact within 8–10 weeks — efficiency +30 to 50%, cost −20 to 40%, adoption proven.
Phase 3
12–36 months

Scale, Govern & Sustain

Where your organization shifts gears — a 12 to 36-month engagement to industrialize, govern and sustain AI at scale across the enterprise, as a long-term partnership with your leadership and technical teams.

  • ▸A dedicated AI governance framework (roles, RACI, standards, security, ethics)
  • ▸MLOps / LLMOps pipelines for lifecycle, monitoring and drift management
  • ▸Enterprise observability dashboards (performance, bias, compliance, cost)
  • ▸Continuous alignment with the EU AI Act, GDPR and internal audit
  • ▸Coaching your internal AI champions to progressively own the systems
OUTCOME Sustained business value, full ownership by internal teams, and a resilient AI ecosystem that improves over time.
